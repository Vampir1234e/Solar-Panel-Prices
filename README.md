Solar panel prices in Pakistan are changing so quickly that checking only one dealer’s quote is not enough anymore.

A small difference of Rs. 2 or Rs. 3 per watt can change the total cost of a 5kW or 10kW solar system by thousands of rupees. Before buying, always compare the panel brand, wattage, grade, documentation, warranty, and city-wise market rate.

The cheapest solar panel is not always the best choice. A-grade documented panels with proper warranty support are usually safer for long-term use, especially for home and net metering systems.
